I am currently a Dual PhD Candidate by the [College of Artificial Intelligence](https://ai.nankai.edu.cn/index.htm) at [Nankai University](https://www.nankai.edu.cn/), under the supervision of [Prof. Yongchun Fang](https://nankai.teacher.360eol.com/teacherBasic/preview?teacherId=10244), and the [Department of Computing](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/comp/) at [the Hong Kong Polytechnic University](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/), under the supervision of [Prof. Jiannong Cao](https://www4.comp.polyu.edu.hk/~csjcao/#). 

Previously, I received a B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology of Honors Program from the [College of Information and Electrical Engineering](https://ciee.cau.edu.cn/), as well as a second bachelor’s degree in Economics with a concentration in Finance from the [College of Economics and Management](https://cem.cau.edu.cn/), both at [China Agricultural University](https://www.cau.edu.cn/).

From May 2025 to November 2025, I was a Visiting Student in the [Department of Computing](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/computing/) at [Imperial College London](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/) in UK, under the supervision of [Prof. Julie McCann](https://profiles.imperial.ac.uk/j.mccann/publications).

My research interests include Embodied Artificial Intelligence, Context-Aware Systems, Robotics Foundation Models, Multimodal Perception and Learning Algorithms. My current work focuses on Interactive Mobile Manipulation (MoMa) for Long-term Robot Autonomy in Home Scenes.
