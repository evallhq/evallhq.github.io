I am currently a Dual PhD Candidate by the College of Artificial Intelligence at Nankai University, under the supervision of Prof. Yongchun Fang, and the Department of Computing at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, under the supervision of Prof. Jiannong Cao. 

Previously, I received a B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology of Honors Program from the College of Information and Electrical Engineering, as well as a second bachelor’s degree in Economics with a concentration in Finance from the College of Economics and Management, both at China Agricultural University.

From May 2025 to November 2025, I was a Visiting Student in the Department of Computing at Imperial College London in UK, under the supervision of Prof. Julie McCann.

My research interests include Embodied Artificial Intelligence, Context-Aware Systems, Robotics Foundation Models, Multimodal Perception and Learning Algorithms. My current work focuses on Interactive Mobile Manipulation for Long-term Robot Autonomy in Home Scenes.
